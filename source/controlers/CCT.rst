CCT Dimmer 2M3P 
=================

2M3P Dimmer works with ESP32-DevKitC. 
You can use it to control 2 bi-color lights at same time.

* Power: Total 150W
* Input: DC 24V; 
* Output: Dimmable 24V
* WIFI: 802.n
* Output Terminal: G16-3 aviation connector
